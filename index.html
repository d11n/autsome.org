Autism

Autism is one of many neurological variants, or neurotypes, that are a natural part of human diversity. There are a broad range of Autistic expressions that include a variety of inner experiences and social behaviors. The characteristics of a person's Autistic expression can differ significantly from the social expectations and non-Autistic patterns of speech, thought, emotion, facial expressions, body language, etc. This can lead to avoidable confusion, misunderstanding, miscommunication, and distress for both Autistic and non-Autistic people who live and/or work together, as well as leads to preventable trauma for Autistic people.
